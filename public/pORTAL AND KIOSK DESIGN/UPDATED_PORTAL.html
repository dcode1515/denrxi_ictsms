About Us

Mission & Vision

Mission: To provide reliable, secure, and efficient information and communication technology services that support the mandate of DENR XI in environmental protection and sustainable development.

Vision: To be the leading ICT support unit in the region, driving digital innovation and e-governance for a better environment.


We manage the region's network infrastructure, develop in-house systems to digitize workflows, and ensure data security across all provincial and community offices.
